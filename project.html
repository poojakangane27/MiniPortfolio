Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a mug.
  2. Add milk and oil, stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave for 90 seconds. Serve warm.
